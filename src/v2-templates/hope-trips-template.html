Hope Trips

An individual has not started living until he can rise above the narrow confines of his individualistic concerns to the broader concerns of all humanity --- Martin Luther King Jr.

Heart4Kids Hope Trips allow those with a heart for helping children to experience bringing immense hope to those who are suffering. If your passion is to volunteer abroad, volunteer in Africa or work with Aids orphans in Africa, our organization welcomes you to partner with us. The trips are life changing experiences, both for the children we visit and for the volunteers who come with us. For 5 years Heart4kids has conducted Hope Trips to Zimbabwe every year. The goal is to let our volunteers see first hand the plight of the children and the difference that they can make in a child?s life.

If you want to see for yourself the lives of the children we try to serve and to personally be involved in their lives, consider joining us on our next Hope trip.

If you are interested in joining us in 2010 please click here to go to the 2010 Hope Trips. If you cannot travel you can support our work by making a donation

Note: Hope Trips are also referred to by many as mission trips. It entails volunteering beyond boarders or abroad to spread hope.
